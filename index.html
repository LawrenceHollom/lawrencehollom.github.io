Lawrence Hollom

I am a postdoc at EPFL, working with Marius Tiba. I recently completed my PhD at the University of Cambridge, supervised by Béla Bollobás. My research interests cover a range of extremal, probabilistic, and infinitary combinatorics along with convex and high-dimensional geometry. My email is lawrence dot hollom at epfl dot ch.
